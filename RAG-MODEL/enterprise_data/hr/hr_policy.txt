ACME CORP — HR POLICY HANDBOOK v2.5 (CONFIDENTIAL)
=================================================================
Effective Date: January 1, 2025 | Last Updated: March 15, 2025

SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY
------------------------------
Annual Leave: 20 days per year (pro-rated for new hires).
Sick Leave: 10 days per calendar year. Medical certificate required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.
Maternity Leave: 26 weeks fully paid, followed by optional 13 weeks unpaid.
Paternity Leave: 4 weeks fully paid within 3 months of birth.
Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family, 2 days for extended family.

SECTION 2: REMOTE WORK POLICY
------------------------------
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Core hours for remote workers: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time.
IT Security Policy compliance is mandatory for all remote access.
Temporary full-remote arrangements (up to 4 weeks/year) require VP approval.

SECTION 3: PERFORMANCE REVIEW PROCESS
------------------------------
Bi-annual reviews: June (mid-year check-in) and December (annual review).
Rating Scale: Exceeds Expectations | Meets Expectations | Needs Improvement.
Salary increments: 0–3% for Meets, 3–8% for Exceeds, 0% for Needs Improvement.
Promotion eligibility: Minimum 2 consecutive 'Exceeds Expectations' reviews.

SECTION 4: COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
------------------------------
Salary bands are reviewed annually against market benchmarks.
Bonus pool: 15% of base salary for VP level; 10% for Manager; 7% for Analyst/Associate.
401k: Company matches 4% of employee contribution. Vesting: 3-year cliff.
Health benefits: Medical, Dental, Vision fully covered for employee; 50% for dependents.

SECTION 5: RECRUITMENT & ONBOARDING
------------------------------
All open positions must be approved by department VP and HR Manager.
Interview process: Recruiter screen → Technical/functional round → HR round → Offer.
Onboarding duration: 90-day structured program with assigned buddy.
Background check and reference check required for all hires.

SECTION 6: CODE OF CONDUCT
------------------------------
Zero tolerance policy on harassment, discrimination, and retaliation.
Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR within 15 days of arising.
Disciplinary actions: Verbal warning → Written warning → Suspension → Termination.
Whistleblower protections apply for good-faith reporting of policy violations.
