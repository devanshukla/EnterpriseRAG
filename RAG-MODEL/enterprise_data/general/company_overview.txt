ACME CORP — COMPANY OVERVIEW & MISSION
=================================================================
Founded: 2010 | Headquarters: 555 Market Street, San Francisco, CA 94105
Total Employees: ~127 globally (as of June 2025)
Annual Revenue FY2024: $20,400,000 | ARR June 2025: $22,400,000
Investors: Series A ($6M) — Sequoia Capital, Andreessen Horowitz (2019)

MISSION STATEMENT
------------------------------
Delivering secure, intelligent enterprise workflow automation that empowers
organizations to operate with clarity, compliance, and competitive advantage.

CORE PRODUCTS
------------------------------
1. AcmeFlow (Enterprise Workflow Automation)
   - No-code/low-code workflow builder
   - 200+ pre-built enterprise integrations (Salesforce, SAP, Workday)
   - 850 active enterprise customers | NPS: 67

2. AcmeInsight (Business Intelligence Dashboard)
   - Real-time data visualization and reporting
   - AI-powered anomaly detection and forecasting
   - 420 active customers | NPS: 72

3. AcmeSecure (Compliance & Audit Management)
   - Automated compliance monitoring for SOC2, GDPR, HIPAA
   - Audit trail management and risk scoring
   - 310 active customers | NPS: 79
